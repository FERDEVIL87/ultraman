Ultraman no Uta (1966)
Ultraman no Uta (ウルトラマンの歌, Urutoraman no Uta, lit. "Song of Ultraman") is the opening for the TV show, Ultraman. Given it's status as the first Ultraman theme song, it has been used various times throughout the franchise, performed by different artists. The song not only was recorded in Japanese, but also in English, French and Spanish for the show's dubbed releases in foreign markets. This song exists in universe, made by the humans in tribute to the Ultras.

Artist: Misuzu Children's Choir and Choru-Stella
Released: 1966
Album: Ultraman Original Soundtrack
